Title: Sound Events for Surveillance Applications (SESA) Dataset
Autor: Tito Spadini

# Classes:
	
0 - Casual (not a threat)
1 - Gunshot
2 - Explosion
3 - Siren (also contains alarms)


# Audio Specs:

- WAV
- Mono Channel
- 16 kHz
- 16-bit
- up to 33 seconds


# Description:

The audio files were obtained from Freesound. The dataset was divided between train (480 files) and test (105 files) folders.
